# 🚀 Quick Start for Commanding Officers

Welcome to COMPASS.

This guide will get your ship set up quickly while also helping you understand how to use the system effectively.

---

# ⚡ 5-Minute Setup (Do This First)

If you only do these steps, you’ll already be ahead of most ships.

### 1. Set Up Your Ship
- Confirm your ship/unit information is correct
- Ensure your name and role are properly assigned

---

### 2. Add Your Crew
- Go to **Crew Management**
- Click **Add Member**
- Enter:
  - Name
  - SCC
  - Rank
  - Status (set to *Active* if applicable)

---

### 3. Record Your First Event
- Go to **Event Tracking**
- Create a new event
- Add attending crew members

---

### 4. Add Promotion Points
- Go to **Promotion Points**
- Assign points to members based on activity

---

### 5. Review Your Data
- Confirm:
  - Crew list is accurate
  - Events are recorded
  - Points are being tracked

✅ At this point, COMPASS is fully operational for your ship.

---

# 🧭 Understanding the System (Why This Matters)

COMPASS is built around one core idea:

> Everything connects back to your crew.

### 🔗 How It All Ties Together

- **Crew Management** → Your foundation
- **Events** → Drive activity
- **Promotion Points** → Measure engagement
- **Awards** → Recognize achievement

If your crew list is accurate, everything else becomes easier.

# ⚠️ Common Mistakes (Avoid These Early)

- ❌ Not adding all crew members
- ❌ Forgetting to update status
- ❌ Skipping event entries
- ❌ Waiting too long to enter data

💡 *COMPASS works best when updated regularly—not in batches.*

---

# 💡 Best Practices (CO-Level)

- Review your roster **monthly before reports**
- Enter events **as they happen**
- Keep statuses accurate
- Standardize how names and ranks are entered

---

# 🎯 What “Success” Looks Like

You’re using COMPASS effectively when:

- Your roster is always up to date
- Events are consistently logged
- Promotion points reflect real activity
- You can quickly answer:
  > “Who is active and progressing?”
