    Promotions & Awards (RC)¶

    As RC, you are the approving authority for all promotion and award submissions from ships in your region. This page covers the regional review workflow.

    Reviewing Promotions¶

    Go to Regional Dashboard → Promotions (or Promotions in the left navigation when in regional view) to see all pending promotion submissions across your region.

    Each pending promotion shows the crew member, their ship, current and requested rank, point total, promotion date, and who submitted it.

    Click Review to open the full submission.

    The review panel shows:

    • Member and ship details
    • From/to rank, points, promotion date, submitted by
    • CO Recommendation (for O-6+ promotions) — whether the CO recommends approval
    • Nomination form answers — the CO's narrative responses to the standard promotion questions

    You can edit individual narrative answers inline if corrections are needed before approving.

    Approving a Promotion¶

    Once satisfied with the submission, click Approve. COMPASS updates the member's rank immediately and notifies them by email.

    Rejecting or Returning a Promotion¶

    If a submission needs correction, you can reject it with a reason (which is sent to the CO) or return it for revision.

    Flag Officer Promotion PDFs¶

    For O-6 and above, COMPASS generates a Flag Promotion Nomination Form — the official SFI PDF — pre-filled with all the member's data.

    The PDF includes:

    • Nominee information (name, SCC, join date, email, region, chapter)
    • Current and requested rank with rank date
    • Relevant titles and positions held since last rank date
    • Compulsory course completions (OTS, OCC, SFDPP)
    • CO narrative recommendation answers

    Download this PDF from the promotion review panel and submit it to SFI Membership Services per the standard process.

    Reviewing Award Nominations¶

    Go to Awards in the regional navigation to see all pending award nominations from ships in your region.

    Each nomination shows the nominee, award, nominator, ship, and citation.

    RC Endorsement¶

    For Order of Merit nominations, you can add an RC Endorsement — a brief statement supporting the nomination that is included in the official submission to SFI.

    Submitting to SFI¶

    Once you've reviewed and endorsed a nomination, COMPASS provides two submission paths:

    Option Description
    Download OoM Form (PDF) Downloads the pre-filled SFI Order of Merit nomination form as a PDF
    Submit to SFI Web Form Opens the SFI web form pre-filled with the nomination data
    Mark as Submitted to SFI Records that you've submitted externally (for tracking purposes)

    The generated PDF is pre-filled with all nominee, nominator, region, and chapter information — reducing manual data entry on the SFI side to near zero.

    Notification Preferences¶

    When approving award nominations, you can set the Notification Preference for the award — whether to notify the member immediately upon approval, or hold notification until the award is formally presented (e.g., at a ship meeting or convention).

    Tips for RC Workflow¶

    • Check the Pending Actions panel on your Regional Dashboard daily — it surfaces promotions and awards waiting for your review across all ships
    • For flag promotions, download and review the generated PDF before approving; it's easier to catch data issues in the PDF view than in the review form
    • Encourage COs to write detailed citation narratives — vague citations slow down the SFI submission process
    • Run SFI Eligibility Sync on ships before processing a batch of promotions to ensure OTS/OCC data is current
