# Glossary

**CO** — Commanding Officer. The ship's captain and primary COMPASS administrator.

**COMPASS** — Crew Operations Management & Personnel Assignment Support System.

**db.sfi.org** — The SFI Members Portal. COMPASS supplements but does not replace it.

**EC** — Executive Committee. SFI's governing body at the fleet level.

**LOA** — Leave of Absence. A temporary inactive status for members who intend to return.

**OCC** — Officer Candidate Course. An academy track for officer advancement.

**OTS** — Officer Training School. Entry-level officer training tracked in permanent points.

**Permanent Points** — Cumulative point total driving promotion eligibility. Earned through events, awards, academy completions, and leadership positions.

**RC** — Regional Coordinator. The regional administrator responsible for a group of ships.

**RCOS** — Regional Chief of Staff. Assists the RC in regional operations.

**Region 3** — The SFI region including USS Veritas, USS Comanche, USS Corsair, USS Zavala, USS Joan of Arc, and USS Battle Born.

**SCC** — Star Cluster Certification (member number). Unique identifier for every SFI member.

**SFI** — STARFLEET International. The fan organization COMPASS serves.

**SFMC** — Star Fleet Marine Corps. SFI's marine auxiliary, managed through a dedicated COMPASS module.

**XO** — Executive Officer. The ship's first officer; shares most CO permissions in COMPASS.
