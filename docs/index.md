# COMPASS Documentation

**COMPASS** (Crew Operations Management & Personnel Assignment Support System) is the official platform for STARFLEET International chapter management in Region 3 and beyond.

## Where do you want to start?

- [CO Quick Start](co-guide/getting-started.md) — New to COMPASS? Get your ship set up.
- [Roster Guide](co-guide/roster.md) — Add crew, manage transfers, update ranks.
- [Promotions Guide](co-guide/promotions.md) — Submit promotions and track requirements.
- [Events Guide](co-guide/events.md) — Log meetings and track attendance.

## What is COMPASS?

COMPASS replaces the legacy Access database with a modern, multi-ship platform giving COs real-time visibility into crew activity and promotion progress. It manages crew rosters, promotions, events, awards, academy records, and announcements.

COMPASS is a **supplemental platform** — it works alongside the SFI Members Portal (db.sfi.org), not as a replacement.
