# Managing Your Roster

Your crew roster is the foundation of everything in COMPASS.

## Viewing Your Roster

Go to **Crew** to see your full ship roster with name, rank, SCC, status, permanent points, and last activity date.

## Member Statuses

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **Active** | Full member, counts toward all reports |
| **Reserve** | Limited participation, still on roster |
| **LOA** | Leave of Absence — temporarily inactive |
| **Inactive** | No current participation |
| **Transferred** | Moved to another ship |
| **Retired** | Left SFI or the chapter |

## Adding a Crew Member

Go to **Crew → Add Member**. Required: SCC, full name, rank, status. Recommended: email address, join date.

!!! warning "Duplicate SCCs"
    COMPASS won't allow two members with the same SCC. If you get a duplicate error, check with your RC.

## Editing a Crew Member

Click any member's name to open their profile, then click **Edit** to make changes.

## Updating Ranks

!!! note
    SFI database rank always takes priority. If there's a discrepancy, db.sfi.org is the source of truth.

## Transferring a Member

1. Open their profile
2. Go to **Actions → Transfer Member**
3. Select the destination ship and confirm

Transferred members show as pending on the receiving ship until the CO confirms.

## Roster Best Practices

- Audit monthly before submitting reports
- Enter new members promptly — don't wait until report time
- Use LOA for members who intend to return, not Inactive
- Don't delete members — change their status instead

## Common Issues

**I added a member but they can't log in.** They need to register at compass.sfi.org separately.

**A member appears on two ships.** Contact your RC to resolve.
