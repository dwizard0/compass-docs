# Awards & Nominations

COMPASS manages the full awards workflow from CO nominations through regional approval and permanent point awards.

## Nominating a Crew Member

Go to **Awards → Submit Nomination**. You'll need the member, award, citation, and date of action.

!!! tip "Write a Good Citation"
    Be specific. "For organizing three consecutive monthly meetings during the CO's LOA, maintaining 90% crew attendance" is far stronger than "for outstanding service."

## Award Categories

| Category | Examples |
|---|---|
| **Service Awards** | Length of service, years of membership |
| **Achievement Awards** | Specific accomplishments, milestones |
| **Command Awards** | CO/XO excellence, ship performance |
| **Recruitment Awards** | Bringing in new members |
| **Academic Awards** | Academy course completions |

Each award's point value is applied automatically to the member's permanent points on approval.

## Approval Workflow

```
CO submits → Regional review → Approved → Points awarded
```

Track pending nominations under **Awards → Pending**.

## Annual Awards

COMPASS manages the SFI annual award cycle separately. Nominations are submitted during the cycle window, reviewed regionally, and EC-level awards are voted on by the Executive Committee. Your RC will announce when the window opens.

## Common Issues

**Award not in the catalog.** Contact your RC — the catalog is managed at the regional/fleet level.

**Points didn't update after approval.** Refresh the member's profile. If still wrong, contact the platform admin.
