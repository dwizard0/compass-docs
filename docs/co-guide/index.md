## Your Role in COMPASS

As CO, you are the primary administrator for your ship's data. Your XO shares most permissions and can act on your behalf.

!!! tip "Keep It Current"
    COMPASS is most useful when data is entered promptly. A roster updated weekly takes about 5 minutes. A roster updated quarterly takes hours to reconcile.
