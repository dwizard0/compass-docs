# Promotions

COMPASS manages the full promotion workflow from eligibility checking through SFI database rank update.

## How Promotions Work

```
CO submits → Regional review → RC approval → SFI DB rank update
```

## Checking Promotion Eligibility

Go to **Crew → [Member Name] → Promotions Tab** to see current rank, time-in-grade, permanent points, and outstanding requirements. COMPASS calculates eligibility automatically.

## Submitting a Promotion

Go to **Promotions → Submit New Promotion**. You'll need the member, proposed rank, a justification narrative, and effective date.

!!! tip "Deferred Promotions"
    You can set a future effective date — COMPASS holds it and applies automatically. Useful for promotions announced at events.

## Approval Workflow

| Stage | Who Acts |
|---|---|
| Submitted | CO |
| Regional Review | RC or RCOS |
| Approved | RC |
| SFI DB Update | Submitted to db.sfi.org |

Track pending promotions under **Promotions → Pending**.

## Flag Officer Promotions (O-7+)

- **O-7 / O-8** — Require Chief of Starfleet (CS) direct approval
- **O-9+** — Require Executive Committee vote and Promotions Director review

COMPASS generates a nomination PDF for these. Your RC will guide you through the process.

## Common Issues

**Member shows ineligible but should qualify.** Check that all event attendance and awards are fully entered — missing data is the most common cause.

**Need to retract a submission.** Contact your RC once a promotion has left ship-level review.
