# Getting Started

This page walks you through your first login and essential setup steps.

## First Login

COMPASS is available at **[compass.sfi.org](https://compass.sfi.org)**.

You can log in with email/password or Facebook OAuth.

!!! note "Account Setup"
    If you don't yet have a COMPASS account, contact your Regional Coordinator. Your account is linked to your SCC number.

## The Dashboard

After logging in you'll see your Ship Dashboard with crew count, pending promotions, upcoming events, and recent award activity.

## 5-Minute Ship Setup Checklist

### 1. Verify Your Ship Information

Go to **Settings → Ship Settings** and confirm ship name, registry, region, and that your name is listed as CO. Contact your RC if anything is incorrect.

### 2. Set Up Ship Positions

Go to **Settings → Positions** to assign command staff (CO, XO, OPS, Department Heads).

### 3. Add Your Crew

Go to **Crew → Add Member**. For each member you need their SCC number, full name, rank, and status.

!!! tip "SCC is the Key"
    SCC numbers link COMPASS records to the SFI Members Portal. Always double-check the SCC before saving.

### 4. Record Your First Event

Go to **Events → Create Event** and log your most recent meeting or activity.

### 5. You're Operational

Once your roster and at least one event are entered, your ship is ready for reports, promotion tracking, and award nominations.

## Common Issues

**I can't see my ship.** Contact your RC — your account needs to be linked to your ship.

**My rank shows incorrectly.** Fix it in db.sfi.org first; COMPASS syncs from there.

**I don't have CO permissions.** Contact your RC or the platform administrator.
