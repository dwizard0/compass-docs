# Awards (RC)

As RC, you are the approving authority for all award nominations submitted by ships in your region. This page covers the full regional awards review workflow.

---

## The Awards Queue

Go to **Awards** in the regional navigation to see all pending award nominations from ships in your region.

Each nomination shows the nominee, award, nominator, ship, citation, and submission date. Nominations are grouped by award category.

---

## Reviewing a Nomination

Click on any nomination to open the full review panel. The panel shows:

- **Nominator Information** — name, SCC, email, and their relationship to the nominee
- **Award Information** — the specific award requested and its class/level
- **Basis for Recommendation** — the nominator's citation narrative
- **RC Endorsement** field — your optional endorsement statement

![RC award review — endorsement and submission options](../img/Awards - Nominate 9 (RC).png)

### Adding an RC Endorsement

For Order of Merit nominations, you can add an **RC Endorsement** — a brief statement from you supporting the nomination. This is included in the official submission to SFI and strengthens the case for approval. While optional, it's good practice to add one for any nomination you're forwarding.

---

## Submitting to SFI

Once reviewed and endorsed, COMPASS provides three submission options at the bottom of the review panel:

| Option | Description |
|---|---|
| **Download OoM Form (PDF)** | Downloads the pre-filled SFI Order of Merit nomination form |
| **Submit to SFI Web Form** | Opens the SFI web form pre-filled with all nomination data |
| **Mark as Submitted to SFI** | Records that you've submitted externally (for your own tracking) |

### The Pre-filled Nomination Form PDF

The generated PDF is pre-filled with all nominee, nominator, region, and chapter information — eliminating most of the manual data entry on the SFI side.

![SFI Order of Merit Nomination Form — pre-filled PDF](../img/Awards - Nominate 10 (RC) - Form.png)

Review the PDF before submitting to confirm all details are correct. If anything needs correcting, return to the nomination and edit before downloading again.

---

## Notification Preferences

When approving award nominations, set the **Notification Preference** for how and when the member is told about their award:

| Option | When to Use |
|---|---|
| **Notify immediately** | Member can be told right away |
| **Hold until presented** | You plan to present the award at a meeting, event, or convention and want to announce it in person first |

This setting is per nomination — you can hold some awards and immediately notify others based on the circumstances.

---

## Awards Workflow Tips

- Check the **Pending Actions** panel on your Regional Dashboard daily — it surfaces all award nominations waiting for your review alongside promotions
- Always add an RC Endorsement for Order of Merit nominations — it strengthens the submission and signals to SFI that the nomination has regional backing
- Use **Hold until presented** for awards you plan to announce at the Regional Summit or ship events — it makes for a better experience for the recipient
- Encourage COs to write detailed citation narratives — vague citations slow down SFI processing and reflect poorly on the submission
- The **Mark as Submitted to SFI** option is useful if you submitted via a method outside COMPASS (email, web form manually) — keep it checked off so your queue stays accurate
