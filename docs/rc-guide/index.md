# Regional Coordinator Guide

This guide covers the COMPASS tools available to Regional Coordinators (RCs) and Vice Regional Coordinators (VRCs).

As RC, you have regional-level access across all ships in your region. Your COMPASS view includes a **Regional Dashboard** in addition to any ship-level Command Dashboard you may have as a CO.

## In This Guide

| Section | What You'll Learn |
|---|---|
| [Regional Dashboard](regional-dashboard.md) | Monitoring region-wide ship health and pending actions |
| [Promotions](promotions.md) | Reviewing and approving promotion submissions from all ships |
| [Awards](awards.md) | Reviewing and approving award nominations from all ships |
| [SFI Eligibility Sync](sfi-eligibility-sync.md) | Syncing crew eligibility data from db.sfi.org |

## Your Role in COMPASS

As RC, you are the approving authority for promotions and awards across all ships in your region. You also have visibility into ship health indicators, membership expiration, and SFDPP compliance across your entire region — not just a single ship.

!!! note
    If you are also a CO, you will see both **Command Dashboard** and **Regional Dashboard** in your navigation. They are separate views — the Command Dashboard shows your ship only; the Regional Dashboard shows all ships in your region.
